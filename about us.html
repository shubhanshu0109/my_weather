About Weather forecasting

Technology to predict the conditions of the atmosphere for a given location and time.

The main motive of precise weather prediction is to be able to give relevant data to the people and organizations that can be used to decrease weather-related expenses and losses and increase societal benefits. Also, keeping in mind safety, protection of life, property, public health, and providing support of economic prosperity and quality of life to the people. Forecasting helps the organization to work efficiently and effectively without wasting resources and making optimum use.
